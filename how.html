The Steps...

Have an accesible design in mind from the beggining

Often times starting with an accesible design is easier than redesigning a non-accessesable interface, sometimes we need to take a step backwards to take a larger step forward. Always keeping in mind that universal design is good design will save you time and energy on the path to designing a good website.

Rely on a variety of accesibility & Design tools

Things like screen readers, w3c html validator,the Wave evaluation tool & HTML5 outliner are your friend in creating an accesible design. There are so many tools out there to assist web designers ensuring they are following best standards and practices.

Programm for Responsivness

A responsive site is not only useful for users who wish to view your site on mobile platforms, but responsive can mean your site reacts to whatever technology it is being viwewd on.

Constantly Evolving

While there are best practices, the internet is still a young place. With new technology rolling out all the time, there is so much room for growth and improvment. Get feedback from users & don't be afraid to experiement!
